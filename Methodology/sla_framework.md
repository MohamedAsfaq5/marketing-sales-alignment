# Marketing-Sales SLA Framework

## Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

| SLA | Owner | Target | Measurement |
|-----|-------|--------|-------------|
| Lead Qualification | Marketing | Score ≥ 80 to pass to Sales | % of leads meeting threshold |
| Lead Handoff | Marketing | Within 24 hours of qualification | Average handoff time |
| First Follow-up | Sales | Within 4 hours of handoff | Average response time |
| Lead Disqualification | Sales | Within 7 days of no progress | Time to disqualify |

## Lead Stage Definitions

| Stage | Definition | Criteria |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **MQL** | Marketing Qualified Lead | Form fill, engagement score > 50 |
| **SAL** | Sales Accepted Lead | BANT-F score ≥ 60, qualifies for outreach |
| **SQL** | Sales Qualified Lead | BANT-F score ≥ 80, budget, authority, need, timeline confirmed |
| **Opportunity** | Active Deal | Formal proposal submitted, negotiation in progress |

## Joint KPIs Dashboard

- Pipeline Velocity (MQL → Opportunity, days)
- Conversion Rates (MQL→SAL→SQL→Opportunity)
- Revenue Contribution by Marketing Source
- Lead Response Time (hours)
- Forecast Accuracy (%)
